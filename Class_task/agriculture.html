Agriculture and water Issues

The rainy season in Chennai is very important for both agriculture and water supply. The heavy rains during the Northeast Monsoon help to recharge lakes, reservoirs, and groundwater sources that provide drinking water to the city. Without this seasonal rainfall, Chennai often faces water scarcity in the summer months. For farmers in nearby rural areas, the rains are a lifeline, as they depend on this water for growing crops like paddy, sugarcane, and vegetables. Adequate rainfall ensures good harvests, while too little or excessive rain can affect farming badly.
